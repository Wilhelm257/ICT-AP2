A Relaxing Place in Grand Mercure Adi Sucipto Yogyakarta

The environment of this hotel that is build in 2017 is full of trees, lots of garden, etc. In my opinion, this hotel had a rating almost 5 stars mostly because of the interior. The interior design in my opinion is very clasic but very good. I also like the interior design in the bedroom because it looks cool. At breakfast, the hotel had a restaurant. The restaurant had menus such as pancakes, bread, gudeg, lots of fruits, egg station, types of rice, different meat, etc. The restaurant was kinda crowded consider that there is a lot of Santa - Laurensia students. The restaurant had faciities such as lots of seats, ac, wifi, service, etc.
